About the project

About ClipMerge

ClipMerge is a frontend-ready video generation workspace for turning a short prompt into a stock-footage video.

01

What It Does

ClipMerge accepts a video idea, extracts useful search terms, finds matching free stock footage, and prepares a finished MP4 from those clips.

02

How It Works

The Flask backend receives the prompt and duration, generates keywords locally, collects clips from Pexels, arranges them into a timeline, and returns progress updates to the interface.

03

Free Stock Footage

ClipMerge uses real stock footage from the Pexels library, so results depend on the availability and relevance of those clips. Highly specific, fictional, or rare scenes may not match perfectly, while more descriptive and realistic prompts generally produce better results.

Workflow

From prompt to video

The interface connects directly to the generation API, showing live status updates while ClipMerge downloads, processes, merges, previews, and serves the finished MP4.

  1. Write a prompt describing the video.
  2. Choose the target duration in seconds.
  3. Preview the generated video plan.
  4. Download the completed MP4 when processing finishes.
